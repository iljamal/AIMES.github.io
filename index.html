Mission and objectives

AIMES is designed to move ideas from theory to practice by pairing AI modeling with high-quality experiments and dependable infrastructure. The aim is to deliver tools that are transparent, reusable, and ready for adoption by the wider research community.

Objectives

  • Define shared evaluation standards across partners.
  • Deliver validated AI models with clear performance limits.
  • Release open, documented software stacks.
  • Train the next cohort of cross-disciplinary researchers.

Work packages

Four coordinated streams turn the vision into deliverables. Update these titles as your plan evolves.

WP1: Data and protocols

WP2: Modeling and AI

WP3: Systems and deployment

Engineer scalable services, workflow tooling, and performance monitoring.

WP4: Impact and training
